WHO WE ARE

Built on Operations & Trusted by the Industry

CrewZen is a B2B event hospitality and campaign management agency established in 2019. We specialize in providing experienced backend operational support for weddings, corporate events and brand marketing activations across India.

With execution experience across 28 states, we are known for delivering reliable, well-coordinated and seamless on-ground operations that ensure every event and campaign runs efficiently. At CrewZen, we believe successful events and campaigns are built on strong operations. Our focus on reliability, coordination and execution excellence has made us a trusted operational partner for agencies, brands and event companies nationwide.

Our Story

Abhishek Parmar

FOUNDER, CrewZen

CrewZen was built with a vision to deliver reliable and professional backend operational support for weddings, corporate events and brand activations across India.

I, Abhishek Parmar began my journey in the events and hospitality industry in 2016 at the age of 16, starting as a promoter anf gradually gaining hands-on experience across hospitality management, logistics, registrations, food & beverage, operations, production, client servicing and team leadership.

Over the years, my dedication and on-ground experience helped me understand the importance of trained teams, smooth coordination and strong backend operations in creating successful events. With execution experience across 400+ weddings and corporate events, along with 150+ BTL and sampling activities nationwide. I established CrewZen to provide seamless operational support that brands, agencies and clients can rely on.

Today, CrewZen continues to operate with a simple belief - great events are built by strong teams working behind the scenes.
